Why go electric?

EV statistics

  • Electric or hybrid vehicles can offer significant reductions in emissions over conventional vehicles. All-electric vehicles produce zero tailpipe emissions, and PHEVs produce no tailpipe emissions when operating in all-electric mode

  • As a result of regenerative braking, hybrid electric vehicles (HEVs) use less fuel than conventional vehicles because their electric-drive technology boosts vehicle efficiency.

  • In the UK, there are 33,281 public electric vehicle charging stations. A total of 6,084 of these are rapid EV chargers.

  • The EV market is expected to continue growing in the UK as it prepares for a ban on petrol and diesel cars in 2030. We believe now is a great time to buy an electric vehicle due to substantial investments in the UK's charging infrastructure.
